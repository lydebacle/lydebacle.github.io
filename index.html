FLEA INVASION

Protect your pup from an infestation.

STORY

Flea Invasion is a mobile strategy game designed not for the faint-hearted. Nasty and disgusting fleas harass your pet. It is up to you ultimately to destroy all the fleas before your four-legged companion dies from the infestation.

A health bar indicates the level of infestation and overall health of your pooch. Pick your attacks and defenses wisely from a selection of cards. Depending on the type of card you choose, the effects of your attack or defense will vary.

GAMEPLAY


In order to win, the player's avatar, HUSKY, must kill off enough fleas for the dog to build up immunity. HUSKY starts with a full health bar that is inversely proportional to the amount of flea infestation.

As levels increase, HUSKY loses overall maximum health which makes it harder to take care of him. Fast reactive play make Flea Invasion a race to save HUSKY from the terrors of bugs.

The player will have eight different cards to combat fleas. Four are treatment cards with limited quantity. The other four are action cards that are limited by cooldown time.


Treatment Cards (limited by quantity)
Flea Comb
Kills 50% random fleas
Bath
Slows down fleas
Human Hand
Kills all fleas tapped in 5 second time frame
Medication
Freezes fleas for 5 seconds when they approach the safety threshold



Action Cards (limited by time delay)
Bite
Kills all fleas in vertical area closest to safety threshold
Scratch
Kills all fleas in horizontal area closest to safety threshold
Lick
Kills all fleas in diagonal right
Roll
Kills all fleas in diagonal left
